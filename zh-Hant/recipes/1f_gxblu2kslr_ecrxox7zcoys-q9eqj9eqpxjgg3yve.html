玫瑰花酒釀饅頭

Recipe Title: ✨玫瑰花酒釀饅頭

原始頁面: https://www.facebook.com/reel/1329190755462005?fs=e&mibextid=wwXIfr&rdid=4INtdTd7eVzMRxpr&share_url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fshare%2Fr%2F1EfXemRAos%2F%3Fmibextid%3DwwXIfr

Google 文件: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1F_gxblU2KSLr_ECrxOX7ZCoYS-Q9eQj9EQpxjgG3yVE/edit

準備: 10 min
烹調: 20 min
總計: 45 min
份量: TBD

材料

  • 面粉 300克
  • 玫瑰花 10克
  • 酒釀 150克
  • 仙人掌果粉 5克
  • 一勺豬油
  • 3克酵母
  • 清水 50毫升

做法

  1. Mix all ingredient to form a dough
  2. Cut into 4 pieces
  3. Each piece form a ball and make a cross cut at the top of the ball
  4. Rest for fermentation for 15 min
  5. High heat to steam for 15 min, turn off the heat and keep the lid on for 5 min. Done
