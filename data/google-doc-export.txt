## Black Sesame Cotton Sponge Cake
Summary: A light and fluffy black sesame sponge cake with a nutty aroma and cloud-like crumb.
Prep Time: 25 minutes
Cook Time: 40 minutes
Servings: 8 slices
Ingredients:
- 6 egg whites
- 6 egg yolks
- 80g cake flour
- 60ml milk
- 55ml neutral oil
- 65g sugar
- 35g black sesame paste
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions:
1. Whisk milk, oil, vanilla, and black sesame paste until smooth.
2. Fold in cake flour and egg yolks to make a glossy batter.
3. Beat egg whites with sugar to medium peaks.
4. Fold meringue into batter in three additions.
5. Bake at 150C water-bath style until the center is set and springy.

## Chicken Adobo Rice Bowl
Summary: Classic Filipino-style chicken adobo served over steamed rice with bright calamansi.
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 35 minutes
Servings: 4
Ingredients:
- 700g chicken thighs
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 90ml soy sauce
- 70ml cane vinegar
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- Steamed jasmine rice
- Calamansi wedges
Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce, garlic, and pepper.
2. Simmer chicken with marinade, vinegar, bay leaves, and sugar.
3. Reduce sauce until glossy and savory.
4. Slice chicken and spoon over hot rice.
5. Finish with calamansi before serving.

## Lemongrass Coconut Shrimp Soup
Summary: A warming coconut milk soup with lemongrass, shrimp, and bright lime.
Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 18 minutes
Servings: 4
Ingredients:
- 350g shrimp, peeled
- 1 stalk lemongrass, bruised
- 400ml coconut milk
- 500ml stock
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp red curry paste
- 150g mushrooms
- 1 tsp chili oil
- Lime juice
Instructions:
1. Simmer stock, lemongrass, and curry paste for 8 minutes.
2. Add coconut milk and mushrooms and bring to a gentle simmer.
3. Add shrimp and cook until pink and just firm.
4. Season with fish sauce and lime juice.
5. Top with chili oil and herbs.

## Roasted Tomato Basil Pasta
Summary: A weeknight pasta with roasted tomatoes, basil, and parmesan.
Prep Time: 12 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Ingredients:
- 320g pasta
- 500g cherry tomatoes
- 4 garlic cloves
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 40g parmesan
- 1 cup basil leaves
- Salt and pepper
Instructions:
1. Roast tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper until jammy.
2. Cook pasta in salted water and reserve some pasta water.
3. Toss pasta with roasted tomato mixture and basil.
4. Add parmesan and a splash of pasta water until glossy.

## Miso Ginger Tofu Lettuce Wraps
Summary: Crisp lettuce wraps with savory miso tofu and ginger aroma.
Prep Time: 18 minutes
Cook Time: 14 minutes
Servings: 3
Ingredients:
- 400g firm tofu, crumbled
- 1 tbsp white miso
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 2 scallions, sliced
- 1 carrot, diced
- Butter lettuce leaves
Instructions:
1. Pan-sear tofu until golden and lightly crisp.
2. Stir in miso, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger.
3. Add carrot and scallions and cook briefly.
4. Spoon into lettuce leaves and serve immediately.

## Mango Coconut Chia Pudding
Summary: Creamy make-ahead pudding layered with mango for a light dessert.
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 0 minutes
Servings: 4 jars
Ingredients:
- 400ml coconut milk
- 4 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 ripe mango, diced
- Toasted coconut flakes
Instructions:
1. Mix coconut milk, chia seeds, and maple syrup.
2. Chill for at least 4 hours until thickened.
3. Layer with fresh mango and top with coconut flakes.
