Fresh onion mousse

Is it okay to have such sweet and delicious vegetables? ? I will teach you a new onion mousse that will blow your mind. [Ingredients] ・4 balls of fresh eggs ・About 4-50g of butter ・Salt ・Gelatin 1-2% of the total amount (depending on the consistency of your choice) ・Fresh cream 1/5 of the puree of new balls *This time, the puree was about 500g with 4 balls, so I used 100ml of fresh cream and 12g of gelatin. #New onion #recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 new balls
  • About 4-50g butter
  • Gelatin 1-2% of the total amount (according to your desired hardness)
  • Fresh cream 1/5 of Shintama puree

Instructions

  1. Add fresh cream to the puree and mix (1/5 the amount of fresh cream).
  2. Add gelatin and adjust the consistency.
  3. Add butter and season with salt.
  4. Pour into a mold and chill in the refrigerator to solidify the mousse.
