清酒煮鮑魚配鮑魚

- Sake - 2 tbsp

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 2 hr 15 min
Total: 2 hr 30 min
Servings: TBD

Ingredients

  • 鮑魚 5隻
  • 昆布 5g
  • 醬油 2湯匙
  • 清酒 2湯匙
  • 味醂 2湯匙
  • 糖 2茶匙
  • 鰹魚湯包 2個
  • 水 700mL
  • 蛋黃 1個
  • 醬油 1湯匙
  • 淡忌廉 1湯匙
  • 糖 少許
  • Abalone – 5 pcs
  • Soup Base Seasoning:
  • Kombu – 5g
  • Soy sauce – 2 tbsp
  • Sake - 2tbsp
  • Mirin – 2 tbsp
  • Sugar – 2 tsp
  • Dashi packs – 2
  • Water – 700mL
  • Liver Sauce Seasoning:
  • Egg yolk – 1
  • Soy sauce – 1 tbsp
  • Heavy cream – 1 tbsp

Instructions

  1. 滾水加入少許紹酒、鹽，灼鮑魚約2分鐘，取出去殼，鮑魚肉和內臟分開，撕走鮑魚咀
  2. 湯汁加入鮑魚，小火煮1小時
  3. 鮑魚肝加到湯汁中煮約5分鐘，取出過篩，加入肝醬調味拌勻
  4. 小火煮肝醬至濃稠，再過篩，完成 。
  5. In boiling water, add a little Shaoxing wine and salt. Blanch the abalone for about 2 minutes. Remove, take out the meat, separate the innards, and tear off the abalone mouth.
  6. Add abalone into the soup base. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour.
  7. Add the abalone liver into the soup, simmer about 5 minutes. Strain, then mix with liver sauce seasoning.
  8. Simmer the liver sauce on low heat until thickened, strain again, and it’s done. ✅
