Ingredients

  • 5 abalone
  • Soup seasoning:
  • Kombu 5g
  • soy sauce 2 tablespoons
  • sake 2 tablespoons
  • Mirin 2 tablespoons
  • sugar 2 tsp
  • Bonito soup dumplings 2 pieces
  • Water 700mL
  • Liver pate seasoning:
  • 1 egg yolk
  • soy sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Light cream 1 tablespoon
  • sugar a little

Instructions

  1. Add a little Shaoxing wine and salt to boiling water and sear the abalone for about 2 minutes. Remove the shell, separate the abalone meat and internal organs, and tear off the abalone mouth.
  2. Add abalone to the soup and cook over low heat for 1 hour
  3. Add abalone liver to the soup and cook for about 5 minutes, take out and sieve, add liver paste to taste and mix well
  4. Cook the liver paste over low heat until thickened, then sift through a sieve to complete.
  5. Ingredients:
  6. Abalone – 5 pcs
  7. Soup Base Seasoning:
  8. Kombu – 5g
  9. Soy sauce – 2 tbsp
  10. Sake - 2 tbsp
  11. Mirin – 2 tbsp
  12. Sugar – 2 tsp
  13. Dashi packs – 2
  14. Water – 700mL
  15. Liver Sauce Seasoning:
  16. Egg Yolk – 1
  17. Soy sauce – 1 tbsp
  18. Heavy cream – 1 tbsp
  19. In boiling water, add a little Shaoxing wine and salt. Blanch the abalone for about 2 minutes. Remove, take out the meat, separate the innards, and tear off the abalone mouth.
  20. Add abalone into the soup base. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour.
  21. Add the abalone liver into the soup, simmer about 5 minutes. Strain, then mix with liver sauce seasoning.
  22. Simmer the liver sauce on low heat until thickened, strain again, and it’s done. ✅
