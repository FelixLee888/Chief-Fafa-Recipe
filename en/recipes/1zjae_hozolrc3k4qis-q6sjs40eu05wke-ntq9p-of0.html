Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 150C and line a cake pan with baking paper.
  2. Whisk oil and milk until smooth, then mix in egg yolks and black sesame mixture.
  3. Sift in flour (and starch if used), then fold gently until no dry pockets remain.
  4. Whip egg whites with sugar (and salt if used) to medium peaks.
  5. Fold meringue into batter in 2-3 additions without deflating the foam.
  6. Pour into pan, tap out large bubbles, and bake in a gentle water bath until set.
  7. Cool before unmolding and slicing to serve.
