decorate your cake

Recipe imported from Google Doc.

Prep: 10 min
Cook: 5 min
Total: 15 min
Servings: TBD

Ingredients

  • 100 ml lemon juice
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 2 1/4 (2.25) tsp agar agar (has to be agar agar, other setting agents won’t work in the same way)

Instructions

  1. Heat lemon juice, sugar and agar agar in a small saucepan;
  2. boil for about 1–2 minutes while whisking to prevent burning.
  3. Pour the gel into a plastic container and refrigerate until the broken gel can set fully.
  4. Transfer the gel to a tall narrow plastic pitcher and blend with an immersion blender until smooth and clump-free.
  5. Heat lemon juice, sugar and agar agar in a small sauce pan. Boil the mixture for approx. 1-2 minutes while whisking to prevent it from burning.
  6. Pour the gel in plastic container and put it in the fridge so the broken gel can set fully.
