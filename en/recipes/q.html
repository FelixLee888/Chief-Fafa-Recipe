Prep: 10 min
Cook: 45 min

Servings: TBD

Ingredients

  • One egg at room temperature
  • Sugar 40g
  • Condensed milk 25g
  • Honey 5g
  • salt 1g
  • Glutinous rice flour 132g
  • Corn flour 30g
  • Warm milk 200g
  • Melted butter 40g
  • Then bake in the air fryer at 180 degrees for 5 minutes. It will be more delicious. 12 butter baked rice cakes

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together until there are no lumps.
  2. Pour into the mold, about 80% full, and divide into 12 pieces.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees and bake for 38 minutes.
  4. If using an air fryer, bake at 180 degrees for 5 minutes.
