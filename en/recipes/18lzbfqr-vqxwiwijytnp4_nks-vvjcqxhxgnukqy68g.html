Instructions

  1. "I like to eat fried noodles with shredded pork, but they must be golden on both sides and have a center in the middle to taste delicious. Most of the fried noodles with shredded pork eaten today are fried, and the middle is tasteless." Celebrity chef Ricky said with emotion.
  2. To make fried noodles with shredded pork delicious, you must first put effort into the noodles. He said the most important thing is to season the noodles with light soy sauce and then fry them. "Use an appropriate amount of oil to slowly fry the surface of the crispy noodles. Don't add a lot of oil to fry it just for the sake of speed." He said with a smile.
  3. He suggested using pork neck instead of lean meat, because after frying, lean meat often becomes dry and soggy, but pork neck is not afraid of these problems.
  4. The secret to preventing the gravy from forming a lump is to mix the chicken broth and cornstarch together first, then immediately pour in the fried ingredients and cook it slowly until it boils. The resulting gravy will not grain and has just the right consistency.
  5. Finally, place the noodles on top of the cooked ingredients. "This way, you don't have to worry about the gravy making the golden noodles on both sides become chewy. Fried noodles with shredded pork that are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside are the way to go," he said while eating.
