A hearty lemon scone

🍋A dripping lemon scone｜Crispy shell + popping filling Share a popping lemon scone. 🍋🍋🍋 The outer shell is slightly crispy, with a little burnt aroma of butter. 🧈 Once it is opened, there is a soft scone in the middle. 🍪 There is also a layer of smooth lemon filling, 🍋 it will flow out when pressed lightly. The taste is very clean, with a refreshing sense of acidity, and the entire scone has a very light texture. Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and with a little bit of liquid filling, this kind of snack is really suitable to make on the go, just right with a cup of coffee or tea in the afternoon.
