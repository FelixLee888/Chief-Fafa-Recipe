Instructions

  1. Make the glaze: in a pan combine 1 cinnamon stick, 2 star anise, 4 cm sliced ginger, 2 tbsp brown sugar, 2 tbsp honey, zest of 1 orange, 2 tbsp white wine vinegar, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 200 ml orange juice.
  2. Bring to a boil and reduce until thick, glossy, and able to coat a spoon.
  3. Add legs, wing tips and crown to a 316 Series Low Casserole pan.
  4. Keep an eye on the breast and remove once it hits 50°C internal (rising to ~55°C as it rests), and rest uncovered in a warm place.
  5. Add 150 ml Grand Marnier and flambé.
  6. Add 500 ml chicken stock, 1 tbsp honey, 1 tsp Dijon mustard, 150 ml orange juice, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. Strain, and finish with butter and season with salt & pepper.
  8. Add a cornflour slurry (1 tsp cornflour + 1 tbsp water to start) - add more as needed.
  9. Stir in 1–2 oranges, segmented.
