honey butter rice flour bread

🍞 Honey butter rice flour bread ●Ingredients (1 loaf of bread mold) ・Rice flour "Mizuhochikara (for bread making)" (290g) ・Honey (50g) ・Salt (7g) ・Dry yeast (5g) ・Lukewarm water (40℃: 210ml) ・Melted butter (20g) ●Recipe preparation. Line a bread mold with paper. Wrap the lid with aluminum foil for frying. Melt the butter at 600W for 20-30 seconds. 1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Once it's all blended, pour it into a mold and wrap it. *If the dough is hard, add a little warm water while keeping an eye on it. 2. Aim for the dough to rise by 1.8 to 2 times its height. OK to leave at room temperature. Preheat the oven to 180℃. *If using the oven's fermentation function, let it rise at 40℃ for about 25 to 30 minutes, then take it out early and leave it at room temperature. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 180℃. 3. Remove the plastic wrap, cover, and bake at 180℃ for 40 minutes. After that, remove the lid and bake at 210℃ for about 18 minutes until the surface is golden brown. Remove from the mold and remove from heat. Store in a ziplock. *If the dough is overfermented (too much fermentation), the center of the dough will dent after baking. #Rice flour bread #Rice flour bread #Gluten free #Rice flour recipe #Bread
