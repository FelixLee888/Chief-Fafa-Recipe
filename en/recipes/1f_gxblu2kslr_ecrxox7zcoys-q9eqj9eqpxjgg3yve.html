Prep: 10 min
Cook: 20 min
Total: 45 min
Servings: TBD

Ingredients

  • Flour 300g
  • Rose 10g
  • 150 grams of fermented rice wine
  • Cactus fruit powder 5g
  • a spoonful of lard
  • 3 grams yeast
  • 50ml of water

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients to form a dough
  2. Cut into 4 pieces
  3. Each piece form a ball and make a cross cut at the top of the ball
  4. Rest for fermentation for 15 min
  5. High heat to steam for 15 min, turn off the heat and keep the lid on for 5 min. Done
