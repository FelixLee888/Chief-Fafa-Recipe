Instructions

  1. 洗淨臘腸與膶腸，切成條。
  2. 選擇新鮮蘿蔔，去皮，切成條狀（約筷子長）。
  3. 將蘿蔔條與臘腸、膶腸、蝦米及各香料與調味料拌勻。
  4. 加入粘米粉與水，攪拌均勻至麵糊狀，確保蘿蔔與粉漿均勻混合。
  5. 準備蒸煮裝置，蒸至糕體結實即可。
  6. Wash and chop the Chinese sausage and liver sausage.
  7. Combine grated turnips with suitable seasonings and additional ingredients.
  8. Mix the flour and turnips thoroughly, ensuring even distribution.
  9. Prepare the steaming setup and cook until firm.
