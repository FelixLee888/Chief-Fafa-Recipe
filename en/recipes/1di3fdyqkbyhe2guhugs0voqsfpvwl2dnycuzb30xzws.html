Braised Sea Cucumber with Scallion

Braised Sea Cucumber with Scallion: Recipe Title: Braised Sea Cucumber with Scallion

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 20 min
Total: 35 min
Servings: TBD

Ingredients

  • Scallion
  • Lard
  • Chicken broth
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Star anise
  • White pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut scallions into sections.
  2. Heat lard in a wok, deep-fry scallion sections until golden, then remove.
  3. In the same wok, add chicken broth, light soy sauce, sugar, and cooking wine, steam for about 8-10 minutes.
  4. Heat another wok with oil, add ginger slices, scallion slices, and coriander stems to make scallion oil.
  5. Blanch sea cucumber in boiling water for about 2 minutes.
  6. Return the fried scallion oil with remaining ginger and scallion to the wok, add star anise, cooking wine, and light soy sauce, stir-fry until fragrant, then add chicken broth to make the base soup.
  7. Add back the soup from step 3, thicken with cornstarch slurry, and finally drizzle scallion oil on the plate.

  14. In the wok, add sugar and caramelize, then add sea cucumber, scallion oil, cooking wine, light soy sauce, and base soup, simmer on low heat for about 10 minutes, then season with white pepper and dark soy sauce.
  15. Add back the soup from step 3, thicken with cornstarch slurry, and finally drizzle scallion oil on the plate.
