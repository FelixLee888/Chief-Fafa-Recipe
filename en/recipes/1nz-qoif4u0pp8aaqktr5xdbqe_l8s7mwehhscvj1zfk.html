rice sticky skewers

``Rice Mochi Skewer'' - Turn ordinary rice into an impressive snack Ingredients · Freshly cooked rice: 1 regular bowl (according to the number of people in your family) · Sugar: 1 tablespoon (to mix with rice) [Sauce ingredients] · Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons · Brown sugar (or regular sugar): as sweet as you like · Chicken soup base: 1 teaspoon · White sesame seeds: 1 tablespoon · Powdered chili pepper: 1 tablespoon · Sesame oil: 1 teaspoon Directions 1. Make the sauce Add soy sauce, brown sugar, chicken stock, white sesame seeds, chili powder, and sesame oil to a bowl and mix well. 2. Massage the rice and add the freshly cooked rice (1 bowl) and 1 tablespoon of sugar. "Kneading, beating, kneading, throwing" - relieve stress as much as you like! Let's do our best to massage until the rice and sugar are completely combined and the rice is chewy and sticky. 3. Shape with a skewer Add a little water to the bowl (to prevent sticking). Transfer the stirred rice to a bowl. Apply a thin layer of sesame oil to the surface of the freezer bag. Place a spoonful of rice in the center of the bag and place a bamboo skewer in the middle. Fold the bag in half and push it out. → If you place the bamboo skewer in the center of the rice, the skewer will be evenly sticky. 4. Grill and coat with the sauce Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan and place rice glutinous skewers over medium heat. Bake until the surface turns golden brown. Pour the sauce from earlier into it and use a brush (or spoon) to spread it evenly over the entire surface. Bake a little more and once the sauce is fragrant and coated, it's done!

Ingredients

  • Freshly cooked rice: 1 regular bowl (according to the number of family members)
  • Sugar: 1 tablespoon (for mixing with rice)
  • Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Brown sugar (or regular sugar): Adjust to your desired sweetness
  • Chicken soup base: 1 teaspoon
  • White sesame: 1 tablespoon
  • Powdered chili pepper: 1 tablespoon
  • Sesame oil: 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, chicken stock, white sesame seeds, chili powder, and sesame oil to a bowl and mix well.
  2. Add freshly cooked rice (1 bowl) and 1 tablespoon sugar.
  3. Bake and coat with sauce.
  4. Bake until the surface turns golden brown.
  5. Pour the sauce from earlier into it and use a brush (or spoon) to spread it evenly over the entire surface.
  6. Bake a little more and once the sauce is fragrant and coated, it's done!
