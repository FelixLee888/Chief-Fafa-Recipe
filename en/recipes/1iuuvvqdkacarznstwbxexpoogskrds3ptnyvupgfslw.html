  • 1 egg
  • Butter 70g

  • Sugar 60g
  • Low-strength noodle flour Cake flour 150g
  • Baking powder 5g

Instructions

  1. Prepare a rectangular baking dish.
  2. Mix in butter that has been softened at room temperature.
  3. Add sugar and mix until homogeneous.
  4. Add the room temperature eggs in two batches and mix.
  5. Add the mashed banana and sifted flour little by little and mix gently.
  6. If desired, add nuts and mix.
  7. Check the ripeness before placing in the mold and baking.
