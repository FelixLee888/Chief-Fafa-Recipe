Coffee Jelly Noodles

Coffee Jelly Noodles | Coffee Omakase Drinks At Home Pt 8 Did you know you can turn coffee into noodles? We’re turning Japan’s famous coffee jelly dessert and turning it into tender coffee jelly noodles. Mix with a sweet and creamy evaporated milk broth and you have yourself a decadent and tasty coffee dessert! Recipe: 500ml chilled coffee 7.5g Agar Agar powder (less for softer jelly noodles, more for firmer) Few grams of brown sugar Evaporated milk Vanilla syrup to taste Top with coffee caviar (recipe in previous video) And enjoy your delicious coffee jelly noodles! #coffee #coffeejelly #coff

Ingredients

  • 500ml chilled coffee
  • Few grams of brown sugar
  • Evaporated milk
  • Vanilla syrup to taste

Instructions

  1. Whisk in agar-agar at a 1.5 ratio.
  2. Add a few grams of sugar.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Simmer for two minutes.
  5. Pour into a thin tray to cool.
  6. Fold and slice the jelly into thin strips.
  7. Serve the noodles in the broth.
