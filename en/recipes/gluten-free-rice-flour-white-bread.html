Gluten Free Rice Flour White Bread

This is how to make one loaf of bread using 100% rice flour. When making one loaf of bread, the choice of rice flour is important. Choose high-quality rice flour with less starch damage. Ingredients

  • Natural Dry Yeast 7g
  • Lukewarm Water 40g
  • Confectionary Rice Flour (KOKU) 350g
  • Cane Sugar24g
  • Sea Salt 7g
  • Lukewarm Water 280g＊you need adjusted
  • Vegetable Oil 28g
  • you need adjusted Vegetable Oil 28g

Instructions

  1. Mix 350g of Confectionary Rice Flour (KOKU), 24g of Cane Sugar, and 7g of Sea Salt.
  2. Add activated yeast water and 280g of Lukewarm Water and combine to form a dough (adjust the amount according to the condition of the dough).
  3. Add 28g of Vegetable Oil and knead.
  4. Pour the dough into a bread mold and let it rise.
